MemeVerse Infinity is an innovative project built on the Alephium blockchain, combining NFTs, Play-to-Earn (P2E) mechanics, and various interactive elements to provide a rich experience for players and community members. By purchasing MVT tokens, users can participate in a variety of games, activities, and community governance, mint and upgrade NFTs, and earn rewards through multiple channels.

MemeVerse Infinity is dedicated to creating a vibrant ecosystem through its unique mechanisms and active community. In the future, the project plans to introduce more games and interactive features to continually enhance the user experience.

Mechanisms

FomoGame Prize Pool Mechanism

In MemeVerse Infinity’s FomoGame, the prize pool mechanism is a core part of the economic system. The mechanism operates as follows: Prize Pool Accumulation and Real-Time Dividend Mechanism.

Fast and Long Game Modes

FomoGame offers two different game modes to meet the needs of different players: Fast Mode (10 mins) and Long Game Mode (12 hrs), Each Key purchase extends the countdown, and the last player to purchase a Key wins.

Countdown Mechanism

The countdown mechanism in FomoGame varies based on the selected mode. In Fast Mode, players have 10 minutes to purchase Keys initially, followed by a 10-minute countdown where each Key purchase extends the timer by 30 seconds. In Long Game Mode, the game starts with a 12-hour countdown, and after this period, another 12-hour countdown begins, with the last player to purchase a Key winning the game..

How it works

MemeFun and MemeBot empower users to Create, Trade, and participate in meme coin projects, with features including easy meme coin creation, secure liquidity pools, rewards for both holders and creators, and practical tools for FomoGame and Alephium-based token trading, all while incentivizing MVT staking for discounts and ALPH rewards.

MemeFun

Create Meme Coins / Liquidity Pool / Airdrop

MemeFun is a core feature of the MemeVerse Infinity ecosystem. It is a fair launch platform similar to PumpFun, providing users with the opportunity to create and promote meme coins. Launching on MemeFun only requires 2,000 MVT, equivalent to 1 ALPH.

MemeBot

FomoGame Assistant / Token Trading Assistant

MemeBot is a practical tool within the MemeVerse Infinity ecosystem, designed for players and users to enhance their FomoGame experience and streamline meme coin and Alephium-based token trading.

MVT Staking & ALPH Rewards

Staking MVT for Key Discounts / MVT Stakers Earn ALPH Rewards

Staking MVT unlocks exclusive benefits in the FomoGame ecosystem, including key discounts and ALPH rewards, incentivizing long-term MVT holding and enhancing its value.
